mission statement
One of our short-term goals is to create a safe place for gamers (and non-gamers) alike, to come and learn about the world of Tabletop Gaming. Gaming is such a intricate, incredibly vast part of our society. It unlocks a level of creativity that was once unimaginable. It curates bonds between strangers... establishes long-lasting friendships... and unites cultures of this world, like simply nothing else can. Our biggest short-term goal is to create a community of people who simply enjoy spending time with one another.
